# Week 03 - HVAC Data and Energy Datasets

Month 1 - Domain Knowledge and Data Understanding

**Topic:** Historical HVAC/Chiller Data and Energy Datasets

**Goal:** Identify high-value historical HVAC/chiller datasets and understand their readiness for analytics,
forecasting, and chiller performance improvement.

## Study Topics
• Time-series data structure
• Equipment and chiller plant metadata
• Weather data
• HVAC/chiller energy and performance signals

## Assignment
Research the following datasets:
• ASHRAE energy dataset
• Building Data Genome
• UCI energy efficiency dataset

Build a dataset catalog including:
• Dataset name
• Number of rows
• Feature count
• Time span
• Granularity
• Weather availability
• Forecasting suitability
• Chiller performance KPI availability (COP, kW/RT, load)

### Deliverables
• Dataset catalog table
• Dataset suitability summary

### Review Questions
• Which dataset is most suitable for forecasting and why?
• Which dataset best supports chiller performance analysis and why?

### Completion Criteria
• Catalog is complete and comparable across datasets
• Suitability notes are linked to your Month 2 modeling goals
• Data limitations and risks are explicitly noted
